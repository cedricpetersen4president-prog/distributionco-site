Our Distribution Channels

We offer a multi-faceted approach to connect your brand with a diverse range of customers.

Channel One

Direct-to-Consumer (E-commerce)

We operate a portfolio of niche e-commerce websites, each tailored to a specific customer segment. By partnering with us, your products gain immediate access to these highly-engaged online communities. We handle the storefront, marketing, and customer service, allowing your brand to tap into new markets effortlessly.

  • Access to targeted, high-intent shoppers.
  • Full management of online sales channels.
  • Data-driven insights from niche markets.
Channel Two

Physical & Event Retail

We bridge the gap between online and offline. Our team secures placement for your products in brick-and-mortar stores and at high-traffic events and trade shows. This hands-on approach puts your brand directly in front of customers, creating memorable experiences and driving in-person sales.

  • Placement in relevant retail environments.
  • Brand presence at key industry events.
  • Opportunities for product demos and sampling.
Channel Three

Marketplace Management

We are experts in navigating major online marketplaces like Amazon, Walmart, and eBay. Our team manages every aspect of your marketplace presence, from listing optimization and advertising campaigns to inventory management and MAP policy enforcement, ensuring your brand stands out and thrives in a competitive environment.

  • Expert management for major online marketplaces.
  • Strategic advertising and promotion.
  • Strict brand protection and MAP enforcement.
